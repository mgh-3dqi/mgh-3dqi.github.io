WBMRI-NF 2023: Neurofibromatosis Tumor Segmentation on Whole-body MRI Challenge


Prize

The top 3 teams will receive cash award (1st place: $1,000; 2nd place: $750; 3rd place: $500). The award certificates will be awarded to all members of the winning teams. These teams will also be invited to participate in preparation of the challenge manuscript to become byline authors.

Teams that obtain the test scores in the challenge leaderboard and complete technical methods and results writeups reviewed by the organizing committee will receive electronic Successful Participate Certificates. These teams will also be invited to be consortium-level authors on the Challenge manuscript.
